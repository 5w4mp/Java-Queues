Queue data Structure:

Concept: 1.) stores arbitrary type of objects.
         2.) Insertion and deletion follows
         3.) First in, First out strategy.
         4.) Think about a checkout line at the grocery store.  or water in a pipe.
Operations on Queue:
         1.) void enqueue(Object e):
            -insert e at the end of the queue.
         2.) Object dequeue():
            -Removes and return the element and the front of the queue.
         3.) Object front():
            -Returns the front element //getter
         4.) int size():
            -how big is the queue.
         5.) boolean isEmpty():
            - is the queue empty.//size == 0?
